WiFi Instructions

Connect to WiFi
  1. Connect to the CNC-Guest Wi-Fi network.
  2. If you have an existing CNC-Guest account:
    • Enter your username and password.
    • Accept the terms and conditions.
    • Click the Sign On button.
  3. If you do not have an existing CNC-Guest account:
    • Click on the link: Or register for guest access.
    • When the screen on the right appears, fill in the details for each field.
    • For the field, For person being visited (email), enter your email address.
    • Click the Register button to create the account open the login page.
    • Enter the username and passcode sent as a text message to your mobile device to connect to the CNC-Guest Wi-Fi.
