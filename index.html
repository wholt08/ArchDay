📸 Upload Your Photos & Videos to Teams

Help us capture the moments! Follow these steps to add your photos and videos directly to our event channel:

  1. Open the Channel: Click this direct link:
    Join the 2025 Architecture Day Channel
    (You might also find QR codes on signage at the event.)
  2. Launch Teams: Your browser will ask to open Microsoft Teams.
    • If you have the Teams app installed, choose "Open It" or similar.
    • If not, you may need to install Teams or use the web version. Log in with your Centene email + network password and complete Duo 2FA if prompted.
  3. Confirm Channel: Ensure you've landed in the correct channel: Data & Analytics Architecture Events → 2025 Architecture Day. If not, try the link or QR code again.
  4. Post Your Photo/Video:
    • Tap "New conversation" (or look for a camera icon).
    • Choose to Take photo/video or Upload from your device/gallery.
    • Add an optional caption to share context!
    • Hit Send ().

Having trouble uploading? Please ping Srinivas Konchada on Teams for assistance!
